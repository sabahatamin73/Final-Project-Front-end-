HUNZA - Where the Mountains Meet the Skies

Hunza is a beautiful mountainous valley located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. The video contains the highlights of my recent trip to Hunza. The video features places of Hunza like:
– Karimabad town
– Aliabad town
– Mt. Rakaposhi (Actually in Nagar, but good views from Hunza)
– The Karakoram Highway and Khunjerab Border (Pakistan-China)
– Attabad Lake
– Passu Cathedral
– Majestic landscapes
